Who are we ?

Our History & Our Commitment

Founded in 2019, the ONGD MWBFG MASTODONS ( Mastodons without border for future generations) has evolved from a local citizen dynamic into a consolidated institution, officially recognized by the Ministry of Environment and Sustainable Development (Certificat n°119/DRCE/SG/EDD/2025).

We empower young people, students, and local communities to provide concrete solutions to climate emergencies and preserve our fragile ecosystems.

Vision, Mission & Values

Our vision

A resilient and eco-responsible congolese society , where youth lead sustainable actions to counter climate disruptions.

Our Mission

Regenerate ecosystems , fight against deforestation, eradicate urban unsanitary conditions, and promote "Green Schools".

Our Values

Integrity , absolute transparency, respect towards our partners, community solidarity, and eco-citizen ethics.
